How It Works

Most ghost boxes use random number generators. Dean_ITC uses Resistance.

Think of it as a Leaky Bucket. The system is constantly draining energy (Cooling). To speak, the input source must generate hits faster than the system drains them. Random noise fades to silence. Only deliberate, sustained intent breaks through.

If the screen is black, it means the line is silent. We value silence over fake noise.

In case you are new to this, Entities/spirits do not respond with yes/no or give you lottery numbers. Things are different on the other side and they 'think' in concepts rather than words.  They are also not 'allowed' to tell you many things due to the 'law of continuity' etc so they speak in metaphors and riddles, hoping you will figure the truth out for yourself. Their response will seem poetic and you will need to read it slowly and carefully to figure out what they are trying to tell you.eg, a response mentioning Rwanda might not refer to the actual country but might refer to civil war instead, given that places history.A response about a 'curtain'  might refer to the 'iron curtain' eg old Russia, USSR etc
